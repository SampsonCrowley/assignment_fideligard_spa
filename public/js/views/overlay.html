Getting Started

Fideligard is a turn-based Stock Portfolio Simulator, where each day is a turn.

You may make as many trades as you want per day before advancing to the next. Once you advance, you cannot go back in time.

You CAN however, view any stock data from any days you have passed by selecting a date from the date-picker. To return to your current progress, click the "Go To Current" button at the top.

To advance to the next day of trading, click the "Advance" button

End Of Game

The Game Ends when you pass the last day of simulation

After the game ends, all of your existing stocks will be sold, and your profits will be calculated

When the game is over, you can look at your transaction history and portfolio, as well as historical stock data. You will not be able to make any more trades.
